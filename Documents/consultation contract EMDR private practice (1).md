## Consultation Contract

The purpose of this contract is to establish a clear understanding of the expectations of consultation. I provide supervision to qualified therapists in private practice, and EMDR consultation to therapists who have completed the standard EMDR training. Consultation can be provided in person or online, and is usually on a monthly basis.

## Expectations and Responsibilities of Consultation

- As a consultant, I do not hold clinical responsibility or liability for a consultee's clinical work. As qualified therapists (and EMDR therapists), they practice within their own professional codes of conduct and ethics, and are accountable for their own practice.
- My role in supervision is to provide a space for the consultee to think through any issues in their private practice and to develop formulations and therapy plans for their clients.
- With regard to EMDR consultation, my role is to help the consultee understand the technical aspects of integrating EMDR into the overall psychological formulation and therapy plan, and to enhance their understanding of the theoretical and practical applications of EMDR. This will also involve exploring and identifying any possible risks or contraindications for using EMDR at a particular time or in a particular situation, in order to enhance the service offered to the client and to ensure their welfare.
- I aim to keep the consultee informed of training, research and new developments within the EMDR and trauma-related field, including identification of ongoing CPD.
- The consultee will be expected to arrive at consultation prepared, having considered what they would like to discuss and any supervision questions.
- If undertaking the accreditation process, the consultee will be expected to submit video recordings of some therapeutic sessions, or arrange for direct observation of their practice, as required by the accreditation criteria.

## Confidentiality

- All professional and clinical issues discussed are confidential and are not to be discussed outside of the consultation session. The exceptions of this are: to prevent the risk of serious harm; or if required to do so in compliance with the law.
- All clients or professionals discussed in consultation are to be anonymised.

## Cancellation Policy

Cancellations by the consultee must be made 24 hours in advance of your scheduled session by email, otherwise the full session fee is payable. Exceptions will only be made on the basis of an emergency.
